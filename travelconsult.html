Recommendations & advice personalized to you

Every consultation is unique and is customized entirely to you and your trip.

Prior to your consultation you’ll fill out a detailed questionnaire that asks you 30 questions about you and your travel partners, your trip, your interests, and your goals for the consultation.

Your expert will use your responses to prepare personalized recommendations and advice in advance.

When you get on your call, your expert will be ready with ideas perfectly tailored to your interests and travel style.
